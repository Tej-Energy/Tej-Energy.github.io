About Me

Powering the Clean Energy Transition

I'm TeJ — a battery engineer and R&D manager working at the intersection of electrochemistry, data analytics, and large-scale energy storage. At Cospowers New Energy, I lead R&D on Li-ion battery systems from cell-level science to fleet-scale deployments.

My work spans OCV-IR characterization, EIS testing, C-rate profiling, and multi-year degradation analysis across India's telecom infrastructure. I've built a Python-based analytics platform with 40+ scripts driving productivity and customer SLA management.

I'm deeply passionate about second-life battery applications, circular economy strategies, and next-generation solid-state battery research — bridging fundamental science with commercial-scale impact.

Open to collaborations, research partnerships, technical discussions, and industry connections in the energy storage space.
